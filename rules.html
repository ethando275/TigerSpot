Daily Game Instructions

  • You will be given a picture taken somewhere on Princeton's campus
  • It is your job to zoom in on the map and place your marker on where you think the photographer was standing when taking the photo.
  • To get at least 1 point, you must at least be within 100 meters of the correct location. The closer you are, the more points you will receive!

Versus Game Instructions

  • Challenge a friend to play against using their NetID
  • Once both players accept the challenge invitation and hit "play", you and your opponent will be given 5 pictures to guess. The catch is now you will be under a 2 minute time limit, so guess before time runs out!
  • The playing format is the same as the daily, just with 5 pictures instead.
  • Once both players have finished guessing, your results will appear shortly to determine the winner!
